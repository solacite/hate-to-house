The Problem (In NYC)

One of NYC’s biggest challenges is affordable housing. Understanding rent burden, eviction trends, and neighborhood costs is one of the most important things to know for aspiring New Yorkers.

The Solution (DA SITE)

An interactive NYC Housing Insights Portal that visualizes real data like housing costs, competition, and eviction statistics in a clear, public-friendly way.

The Problem (In NYC)

One of NYC’s biggest challenges is affordable housing. Understanding rent burden, eviction trends, and neighborhood costs is one of the most important things to know for aspiring New Yorkers.

The Solution (DA SITE)

An interactive NYC Housing Insights Portal that visualizes real data like housing costs, competition, and eviction statistics in a clear, public-friendly way.

The Engaging Element (DA GAME)

To keep users engaged, we will pair the portal with a simple two-player construction-site fighting game called HATE TO HOUSE. Players fight on scaffolding while the loser “falls off the housing ladder,” representing how competitive the housing system can feel.
